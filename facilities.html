FACILITIES

Here you can find useful information about facility rooms.

KITCHEN
  • COMMON ROOM
  • LAUNDRY ROOM
  • STUDY ROOM
credits: Ranszkiewicz, 2022

LOCATION:
11th floor, across from the elevator

DESCRIPTION:
The kitchen is the room furthest away from the terasse, where residents can prepare their meals. It can be booked.
credits: Grundfos Kollegiet, n.d.

LOCATION:
11th floor, across from the elevator (next to the terasse)

DESCRIPTION:
The common room is the meeting place for all the residents, that is fully equipped. You can bring maximum of 6 guests but you cannot book it - for that use the kitchen.
credits: Grundfos Kollegiet, n.d.

LOCATION:
Basement behind the door to the bicycle storage

DESCRIPTION:
The launderette has laundry machines, but also tumble dryers and vacuum cleaners. It has 9 machines that can be used and booked from 6am to 11pm.
credits: Ranszkiewicz, 2022

LOCATION:
Ground floor, across from the main entrance

DESCRIPTION:
In the study room residents can go to escape the noise and many distractions in their own apartments when studying. There is also possibility to print out documents.
