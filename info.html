TRASH

We sort trash, using specific bins for:

  • plastic, metal & glass,
  • glass & batteries,
  • paper,
  • cardboard.

You have to take care of the larger items such as furniture or electronics yourself.

SMOKING

Grundfos Kollegiet is a smoke free dorm.

If you wish to smoke, you are welcome to go outside the front door or to the terrace on 11th floor. Please don’t smoke on the rooftop terrace, since the floor is flammable.
